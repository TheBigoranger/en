Stability

Definition A square matrix $A\in\mathbb{R}^{n\times n}$ is Hurwitz-stable iff. $Re[\lambda_i(A)]<0,\forall i=1,\cdots,n$.

Theorem(Lyapunov Stability): $A\in\mathbb{R}^{n\times n}$ is Hurwitz-stable iff. there exists $P\succ 0$ such that
\begin{gather}
PA+A^TP\prec 0.
\end{gather}
